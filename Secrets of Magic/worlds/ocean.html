The Endless Ocean

The Endless Ocean is a world of mostly oceans. The islands in this world don't get much bigger than Iceland. This world is full of pirates, mermaids, and other deadly ocean creatures.

Important Landmarks

Serpent Ocean
The Serpent Ocean got it's name from the boiling-water spitting gater-like creatures that haunt their waters.
Golden Islands
Located in the Serpent Ocean. Made of 5 major islands and approximately 13 minor islands. The capital island is Hanu.
