Tzel

Tzel is a mountainous world full of forests. The land is constantly being fought over by humans and Kehstics (who are basically werewolves).

Important Landmarks

Ya'arielm (yare-e-elm)
The native land of the Kehstics. It’s very mountainous and covered with forests.
Hatchala
The brothers Paul and Silus built this village for their family and friends outside the Nameless Forest. They had no idea that there was a village of Kehstics inside the forest. They didn’t realize this until the village was so well established that moving would have been a pain. Paul wanted to run the Kehstic’s off but Silus stopped him and the rest of the villagers from doing so.
Haven
Samsen, Seth's father, moved his family to a nice quiet spot in the Nameless Forest to live quietly. He decided to make it a haven for Kehstics, uncreatively called Haven. As time passed, more Kehstics wandered there or were brought there. The village grew larger. When the Hatchala village moved in, Samsen decided to make it a point to bring peace between Kehstics and humans.
