Beadeo

Beadeo is a beautiful world full of flowers, dragons, hills, and valleys. It is the home of the Guardians, people who love exploring and helping others across the universe. They have magic necklaces that they use to travel between worlds and summon weapons made of Light. The Guardians are a Christian people.

Important Landmarks

Mon Flores
This area is very hilly and lushes with tall grass and beautiful wildflowers. Many of the Guardians live here.
The Guardian Temple
This is the temple where young Guardians are taught about other worlds, their magic necklaces, and God.
