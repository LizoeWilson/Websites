Prince Zane

Zane is the reckless prince and soon-to-be king of Auroras. He has a restless spirit and enjoys nothing more than adventures with his friends. Quite often, his reckless nature gets him and his friends into trouble, but he always manages to save them in the end. As careless as he seems, he does have a good heart and strives to do what he believes is right. It's just that, usually, what he thinks is right is act first and ask question later.

  • Age: 23
  • Height: 6'3"
  • Race: Auroran
  • Eye Color: Lime-Green
  • Hair Color: Baby Blue
  • Favorite Color: All
