Seth

Seth is a Kehstic from the world Tzel. When his father died, he took his place as the leader of his clan. No matter what, he will always strive to show kindness to all, even the villagers who are constantly hostile toward him. Seth is a soft, cuddly puppy, but if anyone threatens his clan, he turns into a dangerous beast.

Seth learned Christianity from Patricia's father. Though he doesn't know much, Seth holds tightly to everything he was taught and does his best to live in a way that he believes would make God proud.

  • Age: 20
  • Height: 6'2"
  • Race: Kehstic
  • Eye Color: Gold
  • Hair Color: Dark Brown
  • Favorite Color: Purple
