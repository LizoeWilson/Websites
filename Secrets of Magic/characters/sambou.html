Sambou

Sambou is the fallen prince of a lost world. He made a deal with the demon that now haunts his soul. Sambou has a lot of enemies, which causes him to be very on edge. He has trouble opening up and trusting others, both because he has trust issues and because he doesn't want to hurt someone he's close to.
