Peregrine

Peregrine was the Guardian tasked with taking the only key to the rogue Guardian's prison and wondering the universe. He wondered for years, never settling in one place before finally collapsing and waking up in Origin Point. Even though he lost his memories, he still has a strong sense of morals. Whenever he is brainwashed, he feels terribly guilty and helpless.
