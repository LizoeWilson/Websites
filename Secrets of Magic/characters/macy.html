Captian Macy

Macy is the captain of a band of pirates in The Endless Ocean. She inherited the position after her father was killed by a rival pirate ship. Because she is female, the youngeset on the ship, and not her father, she is constantly under pressure to be a tough and capable leader. This leads her to bury her feelings 24/7. The only emotion she will willingly express is rage and frustration.

Like most Spotted from this world, Macy hates mermaids and blames them for all of the islands' problems.

  • Age: 19
  • Height: 5'7"
  • Race: Spotted
  • Eye Color: Blue
  • Hair Color: Dirty Blonde
  • Favorite Color: Gold
