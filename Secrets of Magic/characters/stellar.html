Stellar

Stellar is the ever-charsmatic Guardian that ended up in Origin Point. Despite his beaming confidence, he has a very big insecurity. For some reason, every time he attempts to use his magical necklace, it drains energy from his body. He has come close to killing himself several times. He often feels helpless because he wants to protect his friends, but always ends up being a liability instead of a help.

  • Age: 18
  • Height: 5'7"
  • Race: Guardian
  • Eye Color: Purple-grey
  • Hair Color: Red-Orange
  • Favorite Color: Red
