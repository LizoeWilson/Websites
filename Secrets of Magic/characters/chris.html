Chris

Chris is an orphan from Technologic. He lives with his two adopted sisters,two adopted brothers, and a kind elderly man who took them all in when no one else would. He is a natural born leader and takes good care of his siblings. Chris is a very warm person and will immediately grow on anyone who meets him.
