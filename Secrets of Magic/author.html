The Author - Azaria Dunston

Azaria Dunston has been writing since before she knew how to write. Literally, when she was a kid, she would write short books using scribbly lines as the "words." A family tradition of the Dunstons is to read a book together after dinner as a family. This, along with her story telling older siblings and the cartoons she loved to watch, established a love of telling stories in Azaria.

The story of Secrets of Magic was first conceptualized in 2013. Azaria wanted to tell a story involving multiple worlds and characters. It started out as a story about a young teenage girl going on adventures in other worlds with fairies and pet monsters. Over the years, the story drastically changed, but the core remained and morphed into the current plot.
