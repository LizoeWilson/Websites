Welcome to the official website for the book series Secrets of Magic!

Secrets of Magic is a book series that Azaria Dunston has been working on since 2013. Will she ever end up actually finishing writing them? Who knows! But Azaria got too carried away with world building, which means that there is plenty of content to make a website for!

Note: This site is lacking much of the art that was intended to be seen. For now, there are place holder images to give an idea of what the completely finished site would look like.
