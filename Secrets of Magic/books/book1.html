Secrets of Magic

Patricia is far from an ordinary girl. With her magical necklace that she inherited from her father, she can travel to other worlds with dragons, werewolves, and mermaids. As the descendent of Guardians, she is expected to help keep peace among the worlds. However, with Prince Zane starting a war in his world, werewolves being hunted in another, and mermaids stealing treasure from pirates, will Patricia be able to handle it all on her own?
