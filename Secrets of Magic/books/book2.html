Secrets of Magic: Origin Point

Chris was living a normal life in the world of technology. One day, he finds himself sucked into a portal and transported to a strange world between worlds called Origin Point. After befriending seven other boys, all from different worlds, they are thrown into a tournament to fight for their lives and win the chance to go back home. But with one of them keeping a dark secret, will they be able to survive the tournament together?
